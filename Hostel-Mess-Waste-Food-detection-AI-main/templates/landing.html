Capabilities

Built for the entire mess ecosystem.

From student check-in to admin analytics — every layer of mess management covered.

01
Smart Attendance
Students mark meal preferences before the window closes. Live counts help the kitchen prepare precisely the right quantity.
02
AI Waste Detection
A trained model predicts food waste patterns from attendance history, giving admin data-backed portion guidance.
03
QR Code Login
Students scan a QR code to check in instantly — no typing, no friction. Works on any smartphone.
04
Timed Meal Windows
Attendance opens only during active meal hours. No late entries, no duplicates — clean data every time.
05
Admin Dashboard
Manage menus, view live attendance, export CSV reports, and consult an AI assistant — from one control panel.
06
Role-Based Access
Three admin tiers — Staff, Manager, Superadmin — each scoped to appropriate permissions and data views.
Process

Four steps.
Zero complexity.

Designed for students who have two seconds and admins who need reliable data.

1
Student logs in
Enter your name and PIN, or scan a QR code. You're on the attendance dashboard in under five seconds.
2
Mark your meal choices
Tap to confirm which meals you'll eat today. Attendance only opens during the active window for that meal.
3
Admin sees it live
The mess team views real-time attendance counts and adjusts food preparation quantities accordingly.
4
AI learns and predicts
The prediction model learns historical patterns and gives increasingly accurate waste-reduction recommendations over time.
